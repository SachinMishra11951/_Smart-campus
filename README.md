# Smart Campus Project: Complaint & Resource Management System

The **Smart Campus Project** is a comprehensive digital ecosystem designed to unify and optimize daily institutional operations. By bridging physical facilities with modern web and mobile applications, this platform eliminates traditional administrative bottlenecks, automates workflow coordination, and maximizes resource utilization.

---

## 📝 Complaint Management Module

A robust, transparent grievance system that empowers students and staff to report, track, and resolve campus issues efficiently.

* **Multi-Channel Submission:** Report facility, IT, or hostel maintenance issues instantly via mobile or web forms.
* **Real-Time Tracking:** View active progress bars (Pending, In-Progress, Resolved) with automated email/SMS status alerts.
* **Rule-Based Escalation:** Automatically routes tickets to specific departments and triggers supervisor alerts if resolution deadlines are missed.
* **Quality Feedback Loop:** Enables users to rate resolution quality, ensuring high standards for campus services.

---

## 🛠️ Resource Management Module

A centralized dashboard to monitor, schedule, and reserve physical and digital institutional assets seamlessly.

* **Live Facility Booking:** Check real-time availability calendars and instantly reserve seminar halls, labs, or sports gear.
* **Inventory Tracking:** Monitor stock levels, physical condition, and routine maintenance schedules for high-value equipment.
* **Utilization Analytics:** Generates usage reports to help administrators identify underutilized spaces and plan budgets.

---

Tailored, secure access controls for distinct user groups across the institution:

### 1. Student / Staff Interface

### 3. Administrator Portal
