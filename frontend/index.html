Campus Workflow Timeline

This section shows how the system can be used throughout a normal academic day.

08:30 AM

Morning checks

Admin reviews pending complaints, lab booking requests and important announcements before classes begin.

11:00 AM

Student requests

Students raise classroom, Wi-Fi, hostel, library and transport issues with category and priority.

02:00 PM

Resource booking

Departments book classrooms, labs, projectors and seminar halls based on available time slots.

05:00 PM

Reports and closure

Resolved items are closed, unresolved tasks are escalated and statistics are updated for tomorrow.
