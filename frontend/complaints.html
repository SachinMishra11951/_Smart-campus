Complaint Life Cycle

Every complaint moves through a simple visible process, so students know what is happening and admins know what to do next.

Step 1

Submitted

Student enters category, priority, title and full details. The system creates a complaint ID.

Step 2

Assigned

Admin checks the category and sends the task to the correct department or staff member.

Step 3

In Progress

Status updates are visible to the student and important delays can be escalated.

Step 4

Resolved

The complaint is closed after solution confirmation and can be used in monthly reports.
